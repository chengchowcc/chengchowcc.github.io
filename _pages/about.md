Welcome! I'm **Cheng Chow**, a PhD student in Social Work at the [Steve Hicks School of Social Work](https://socialwork.utexas.edu/), The University of Texas at Austin, and a Graduate Trainee at the [Population Research Center](https://liberalarts.utexas.edu/prc/). My work sits at the intersection of social policy and immigrant health.

---

### About My Research

Broadly, my research focuses on:
- **Immigration** and **health disparities**
- **Structural racism** and **social determinants of health**
- **Global social work** and **policy integration**

I primarily work with immigrant and refugee populations, racial/ethnic minorities, and Asian American communities using **mixed methods**, **causal inference**, and **data science** approaches.

---

### Recent Work Highlights
- **Principal Investigator**: *Exploring Digital Health Literacy in Asian Communities in Austin* (Health Communication Scholars Program, 2025)
- **Research Coordinator**: *Primary Healthcare Needs of Asian Americans in Austin* (St. David’s CHPR, 2024)
- Published in *Social Science & Medicine*, *Health Policy*, *Sociology Compass*, *Journal of Ethnic and Migration Studies*, and more.

### Engagement & Teaching

I've presented research at SSWR, APHA, ASA, and international conferences in the U.S., Europe, and Asia. My teaching interests include:

- Social Policy & Research Methods
- Advanced Statistical Modeling
